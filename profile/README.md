## Who We Are

Raintree Technology builds at the intersection of developer tooling and agentic AI infrastructure. We ship products that are genuinely useful to humans while simultaneously advancing the open-source ecosystem that AI agents rely on тАФ from CLI frameworks and crawlers to blockchain analytics and financial automation.

Our philosophy is simple: write code that earns its place. Every tool we release is designed with a clear problem in mind, a sharp interface, and zero unnecessary complexity. We favor composability over monoliths, correctness over cleverness, and documentation that actually explains the *why* тАФ not just the *what*.

Our current focus spans three domains: **agent-first developer tooling** (frameworks, MCP servers, auditing CLIs), **financial infrastructure** (perpetual futures automation, on-chain analytics), and **content pipelines** (Markdown conversion, RAG optimization, structured data extraction).
